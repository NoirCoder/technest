# Content Creation Guide for TechNest

This guide will help you create high-quality, SEO-optimized tech reviews that convert readers into buyers.

## Article Structure

Every great tech review follows this structure:

### 1. Title (H1)
- Include the product name
- Add benefit or hook
- 60 characters max for SEO

**Examples:**
- ‚úÖ "Logitech MX Master 3S Review: The Ultimate Productivity Mouse"
- ‚úÖ "Keychron Q1 Pro Review: Premium Mechanical Keyboard Under $200"
- ‚ùå "Mouse Review" (too vague)

### 2. Introduction
- Hook the reader (problem/desire)
- Mention the product immediately
- Set expectations for the review
- 2-3 paragraphs

### 3. Quick Verdict (Optional)
A TL;DR box with:
- Star rating
- Price
- Best for: [audience]
- Key pros and cons

### 4. Main Content

#### Sections to Include:
- **Design & Build Quality** - Materials, aesthetics, ergonomics
- **Features & Specifications** - Tech specs, what's included
- **Performance** - Real-world testing, benchmarks
- **User Experience** - Day-to-day use, comfort, workflow
- **Comparison** - vs. competitors at similar price
- **Price & Value** - Is it worth it?
- **Final Thoughts** - Recommendation and who should buy

### 5. Conclusion
- Summary of key points
- Clear recommendation
- Call to action with affiliate link

## Writing Tips

### Be Specific and Detailed
‚ùå "The keyboard feels great to type on."
‚úÖ "The PBT keycaps have a pleasant texture, and the pre-lubed stabilizers eliminate rattle on the spacebar and larger keys. After 8 hours of typing, my fingers felt less fatigued compared to my previous membrane keyboard."

### Use Headers Strategically
- H1: Title only (one per page)
- H2: Main sections
- H3: Subsections
- H4: Minor details

This creates a good table of contents and helps SEO.

### Include Visuals
Use external image URLs:
- Product photos from manufacturer
- Amazon product images
- Official press images

### Write for Your Audience
Your readers are:
- Ready to buy (high purchase intent)
- Researching before clicking "add to cart"
- Want honest, detailed information
- Comparing multiple products

## SEO Optimization

### Keywords
Target 1 main keyword:
- "product name + review"
- Example: "keychron q1 pro review"

Include naturally in:
- Title (H1)
- First paragraph
- At least one H2
- Meta description
- Image alt text

### Meta Fields
Fill these in the admin panel:

**Meta Title** (50-60 chars):
`Logitech MX Master 3S Review (2026) | TechNest`

**Meta Description** (150-160 chars):
`Detailed review of the Logitech MX Master 3S. We tested ergonomics, battery life, and productivity features. Is it worth $100?`

### Internal Linking
Link to 2-3 related posts:
- Same category (e.g., other mice reviews)
- Alternative products
- Comparison guides

Example:
`If you prefer a wireless keyboard, check out our [Logitech MX Keys review](/blog/logitech-mx-keys-review).`

## Affiliate Link Best Practices

### When to Use Affiliate Blocks
- After the introduction
- After detailed feature sections
- In comparison tables
- At the end (CTA)

### Be Transparent
Always disclose:
- We earn from qualifying purchases
- Our opinion is honest and unbiased
- We only recommend products we've tested

(Disclosure is auto-added to affiliate blocks and footer)

### Link Placement
Link the product:
- 2-3 times per 1000 words
- Naturally in context
- In affiliate blocks
- In conclusion CTA

## Content Checklist

Before publishing, ensure:

- [ ] Title is compelling and includes product name
- [ ] Excerpt summarizes the review in 1-2 sentences
- [ ] Featured image is high-quality product photo
- [ ] Content is 1500+ words (for detailed reviews)
- [ ] At least 3-4 H2 sections
- [ ] Includes personal testing/experience
- [ ] Has 2-3 affiliate link placements
- [ ] Links to 2-3 related posts
- [ ] Meta title and description filled
- [ ] Selected relevant category
- [ ] Proofread for typos
